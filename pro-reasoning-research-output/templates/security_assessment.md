# Security Assessment Template

## Overview

Summarize the system, question, and overall risk.

## Scope And Assumptions

State what was and was not assessed.

## Threat Model

Identify assets, actors, trust boundaries, entry points, and assumptions.

## Attack Surface

List exposed interfaces, dependencies, data flows, permissions, and operational paths.

## Vulnerability Candidates

Prioritize likely weaknesses without unnecessary exploit detail.

## Impact

Explain confidentiality, integrity, availability, privacy, and business effects.

## Exploitability

Estimate likelihood and required conditions.

## Detection Points

List logs, alerts, metrics, and review signals.

## Mitigations

Provide practical controls and verification steps.

## Residual Risk

State what remains after mitigation.

## Unknowns

List evidence needed for higher confidence.
