# Decision Memo Template

## Recommended Decision

State the decision and confidence.

## Background

Summarize the user goal and constraints.

## Options

List realistic alternatives.

## Comparison

| Criterion | Option A | Option B | Option C | Comment |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Rationale

Explain the key reasons, trade-offs, and evidence.

## Counterarguments

Show the strongest reason the recommendation could be wrong.

## Risks

Prioritize risks and mitigations.

## Execution Plan

List the next concrete steps.

## Revisit Conditions

State what new information should change the decision.
