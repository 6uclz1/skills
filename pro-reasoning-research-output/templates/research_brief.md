# Research Brief Template

## Conclusion

State the answer in 2 to 5 sentences.

## Assumptions

List only assumptions that affect the answer.

## Confirmed Facts

Use sourced bullets for current, disputed, or vendor-specific claims.

## Analysis

Explain what the facts mean for the user's goal.

## Recommendation

Give the practical recommendation, including conditions if needed.

## Uncertainty And Caveats

State unresolved issues, source conflicts, dates, version limits, and confidence.

## References

List only sources actually used in the answer.
