# Citation Examples

## Good

"The vendor states that feature X is available in version Y [citation]. Because your deployment is on version Z, this only applies if you upgrade."

Why it works:

- The citation supports the exact product claim.
- The recommendation is separated from the cited fact.
- Scope and version are explicit.

## Bad

"Feature X is supported. Sources: [long source list]"

Why it fails:

- The source is not attached to the claim.
- Version context is missing.
- The reader cannot audit the evidence quickly.
